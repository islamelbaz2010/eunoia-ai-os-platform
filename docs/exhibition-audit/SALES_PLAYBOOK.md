## How to Use This Playbook

Different buyers have different pain points. This playbook gives you the right opening statement, the right feature to demo first, the right objection responses, and the right closing ask for each industry. Do not use a generic pitch. Personalize based on who's standing in front of you.

## VERTICAL 1: Hotels & Luxury Resorts

### Who You're Talking To
- General Manager (decision maker, cares about brand consistency and cost)
- Front Desk Manager (user, cares about making their shift easier)
- F&B Manager (user, cares about menu accuracy and upsell)
- HR/Training Manager (champion, cares about onboarding speed)

### Opening Statement
> "Right now, your new front desk hire is messaging the GM on WhatsApp to ask about check-in policy. Your GM is answering the same question for the third time this week. What if every team member had instant, cited answers from your actual policies — in Arabic or English — 24/7?"

### Primary Pain Points to Address
- Staff turnover requires constant retraining
- Inconsistent guest experiences because policies aren't accessible
- WhatsApp-based knowledge sharing is uncontrolled and unreliable
- GMs and department heads spend hours answering repetitive questions

### Features to Lead With
1. **RAG Assistant** — Demo with a hotel-specific question: "What is our policy for upgrading a VIP guest who arrives before check-in time?"
2. **Knowledge Base** — Show uploading an SOP and getting an immediate AI answer
3. **CRM Pipeline** — Show managing corporate accounts and travel agents through pipeline stages

### ROI Framing for Hotels
> "A 5-star GM costs $5,000/month. If Eunoia saves 1 hour per day of that manager's time answering staff questions, you recover the cost of the Pro plan in 2 days of work. The rest is pure savings."

### Objections and Responses

**"We already use a WhatsApp group for this."**
> "WhatsApp is where knowledge goes to die. Your SOPs are buried in chat history. A new hire can't search them. The AI can't learn from them. Eunoia is where knowledge becomes searchable, citable, and always up-to-date."

**"Our operations team won't adopt new software."**
> "The interface is simpler than WhatsApp. You ask a question, you get an answer. There's nothing to learn. Most hotels have their first team member using it within an hour."

**"We're already using a PMS for all of this."**
> "PMS manages reservations. Eunoia manages knowledge. They don't overlap. Your PMS doesn't answer 'What's the procedure for a late check-out VIP?' — Eunoia does."

### Closing Ask
> "Let me do a 30-minute demo next week with your actual F&B menu and check-in SOP. I'll show you exactly how the answers look for your team. No commitment."

## VERTICAL 2: Clinics & Medical Centers

### Who You're Talking To
- Clinic Director (decision maker)
- Office Manager (champion)
- Receptionist / Front Desk (user)
- Medical Staff (secondary user)

### Opening Statement
> "Every day, your receptionist is interrupted with the same question: 'What documents does the patient need for this insurance provider?' Or 'What's the procedure for this appointment type?' What if every staff member had the right answer, sourced directly from your clinic's protocols, in under 5 seconds?"

### Primary Pain Points
- Compliance documentation needs to be accessible but controlled
- Staff turnover in receptions is high — retraining costs time and money
- Insurance procedure complexity creates errors and delays
- Patient instructions need to be consistent across all staff

### Features to Lead With
1. **RAG Assistant** — "What documents does a patient need to submit for Bupa insurance claims?"
2. **Knowledge Base** — Upload insurance procedure guides, patient protocols, appointment SOPs
3. **Audit Logs** — Show who accessed which protocol, when (important for compliance)

### ROI Framing for Clinics
> "One incorrect insurance claim rejection costs you more than a year of this subscription. Consistent, accurate protocol knowledge prevents rejection. Eunoia is the cheapest compliance tool you can buy."

### Objections and Responses

**"This could affect HIPAA/patient data compliance."**
> "No patient data ever enters Eunoia. You only upload protocols and procedures — your team's operating knowledge, not patient records. The AI answers questions about how to handle patients, not about individual patients."

**"Our doctors won't use this."**
> "This is for your administrative staff and receptionists — not for clinical decision-making. Doctors answer clinical questions. Eunoia answers 'What's the pre-appointment preparation for an MRI?' questions."

**"We have an intranet for this."**
> "Intranets require staff to know where to look. Eunoia lets them ask a question in natural language and get the exact answer with a source link. It's the difference between searching a document and asking a colleague."

### Closing Ask
> "Upload your appointment type protocols and insurance guides into a trial account. Test it with 3 real questions your reception team asked this week. If the answers are right, we have a deal."

## VERTICAL 3: Real Estate Agencies

### Who You're Talking To
- Agency Director (decision maker)
- Sales Manager (champion)
- Sales Agents (users)
- Property Manager (secondary user)

### Opening Statement
> "Your agents are in meetings, on calls, showing properties — and they're calling the office to ask about payment plans, developer terms, or maintenance procedures. What if every agent had that information in their pocket, sourced from your actual listings and contracts, in 10 seconds?"

### Primary Pain Points
- Agents need quick access to property specifications, payment plans, and developer terms
- CRM data is inconsistent — agents track leads in WhatsApp, Excel, and memory
- New agent onboarding requires months to learn the portfolio
- Developer relations require detailed, accurate information

### Features to Lead With
1. **CRM Pipeline** — "Here's how you manage your pipeline: Lead → Viewing Scheduled → Offer Made → Contract Signed → Completed." Drag and drop between stages live.
2. **RAG Assistant** — "What's the payment plan for Unit 4B in Marina View Tower?"
3. **Contact Activities** — Show logging a call, adding a note, scheduling a follow-up

### ROI Framing for Real Estate
> "One missed follow-up costs a commission. One wrong payment plan figure loses a deal. Eunoia prevents both — every agent, always accurate, every call logged."

### Objections and Responses

**"We already use a CRM."**
> "Which one? If it doesn't have AI that answers 'What are the developer terms for this project?' from your actual contracts, then you have two products to buy instead of one."

**"Our agents don't want to change tools."**
> "They don't have to change. Eunoia is the AI layer on top of what they already know. They still make calls, still do viewings. They just have better information in their hands."

**"What about our developer relationships and exclusive listings?"**
> "All private to your organization. No other agency can see your listings or client data. Row-level security isolates your data completely."

### Closing Ask
> "Give me your top 5 frequently asked questions from agents. I'll show you Eunoia answering all 5 from your own documents. If it works, we start your trial today."

## VERTICAL 4: Restaurants & F&B Operations

### Who You're Talking To
- Restaurant Owner / F&B Director (decision maker)
- Restaurant Manager (champion)
- Floor Staff / Servers (users)
- Kitchen Staff (secondary user)

### Opening Statement
> "Your server is at the table and the guest asks: 'Does this dish contain nuts? Is it halal? What wine pairs with the sea bass?' They don't know. They go to the kitchen. They come back with an imprecise answer. What if every server had your complete menu — ingredients, allergens, wine pairings — in their pocket, and could answer in 5 seconds?"

### Primary Pain Points
- Menu knowledge is uneven across staff, especially seasonal items
- Allergen and dietary information must be accurate — liability risk
- New staff need weeks to learn the menu
- Specials and pricing changes need to reach all staff instantly

### Features to Lead With
1. **RAG Assistant** — "Does the grilled salmon contain any dairy? What are the vegan options on the main menu?"
2. **Knowledge Base** — Show uploading the menu PDF as text, getting instant accurate answers
3. **Team Settings** — Show how a manager updates the menu and all staff immediately have accurate answers

### ROI Framing for Restaurants
> "One allergen incident costs you six figures in liability. One server who can answer a dietary question confidently keeps the table. Eunoia is the cheapest insurance you can buy for your service quality."

### Objections and Responses

**"Our menu changes daily — how does this work?"**
> "Update the document, and every AI answer updates immediately. It's faster than a printed menu update and reaches every staff member's device."

**"We have kitchen display systems for this."**
> "KDS manages orders. Eunoia manages knowledge. 'What's the table's order?' goes to KDS. 'What's in the bouillabaisse?' goes to Eunoia."

**"Staff won't use their phones on the floor."**
> "They're already using their phones. This gives them a reason to that improves service instead of distracting from it."

### Closing Ask
> "Let me upload your menu right now, in this booth. I'll have you asking it questions in 10 minutes. If your server could answer better with this, you have your answer."

## VERTICAL 5: Travel Agencies

### Who You're Talking To
- Agency Owner (decision maker)
- Sales Manager (champion)
- Travel Consultants (users)

### Opening Statement
> "A client calls and asks: 'What's the visa requirement for an Egyptian passport holder traveling to France with a Schengen visa issued by Germany?' Your consultant doesn't know off the top of their head. They Google it. They get it wrong. They call back. What if the answer came from your agency's own verified procedures in 3 seconds?"

### Primary Pain Points
- Visa regulations and travel requirements change frequently
- Consultant knowledge is uneven — senior staff know everything, junior staff know nothing
- Client errors due to incorrect information create disputes and chargebacks
- Tour packages are complex — pricing, inclusions, and supplier terms need to be accurate

### Features to Lead With
1. **RAG Assistant** — "What documents does a Saudi national need for a UK tourist visa?"
2. **Knowledge Base** — Show uploading visa guides, package terms, supplier contracts
3. **CRM** — Show managing client relationships through the booking pipeline

### ROI Framing for Travel Agencies
> "One incorrect visa advice that leads to a denied boarding costs you the client, the commission, and possibly a legal dispute. The Starter plan costs less than one flight change fee."

### Objections and Responses

**"Travel regulations change constantly. How do we keep it updated?"**
> "You update the document when the regulation changes. Same as updating any internal guide — except now the AI answers from the latest version immediately."

**"We use GDS systems for booking. Does this integrate?"**
> "Not yet. Eunoia is your knowledge layer — procedures, policies, visa requirements, package inclusions. GDS handles booking. They complement each other."

**"Our clients want to speak to a person, not a bot."**
> "Your consultants speak to clients. Eunoia speaks to your consultants. The client never sees the AI — they just notice that your consultant knows the answer instantly."

### Closing Ask
> "What are the 5 questions your consultants ask each other most often? Upload those answers to Eunoia in 10 minutes. If it knows them accurately, you have your proof of concept."
