## The 5-Minute Demo Script

### Minute 0:00–0:30 — The Hook (Landing Page)

Start on the landing page. Point at the animated chat mockup in the hero:

> "Hotels today manage knowledge through WhatsApp groups and printed binders. A new staff member joins and spends the first week asking senior colleagues basic questions. The manager answers the same question twenty times. We built the solution."

Click "Start Free Trial" — but don't sign up. Switch to the pre-loaded demo account.

---

### Minute 0:30–2:00 — The Show-Stopper (AI Chat)

Navigate to: **Dashboard → AI Assistant**

The chat now shows suggested questions as clickable chips.

Click: **"What is the check-in procedure for VIP guests?"**

> "I'm going to ask the assistant something a front desk team member asks every shift."

Watch the sources appear first (show them):
> "See these? These are the exact paragraphs from our VIP Protocol document that the AI found. 94% confidence match. It shows you WHERE it got the answer."

Watch the streaming response:
> "The answer is streaming in real time. This isn't a waiting screen — it's generating word by word, just like a colleague thinking out loud."

Ask a second question by typing:
> "Does the salmon on the Terrace menu contain dairy?"

> "I just asked a different question — this time from the F&B menu. Different document. Same answer quality. Every department's knowledge in one place."

**This is the moment. Let them process it.**

---

### Minute 2:00–3:00 — The CRM Demo

Navigate to: **Dashboard → CRM → Pipeline**

> "The AI Knowledge Base is for operations. This is for revenue."

Point at the pipeline board with your 6 demo contacts:
> "Every lead, travel agent, corporate account — managed here. Drag from Lead to Qualified. Every move is logged."

Click on "Dubai Hospitality Group":
> "Timeline of every touchpoint. Calls, meetings, emails, notes. One view for every interaction."

Navigate back, show the search:
> "Search, filter by stage, export to CSV. Import your existing contacts. Same day."

## Handling the Hard Questions

**"What if the AI gives wrong information?"**
> "It only reads from your documents. If a document says check-in is at 2pm, the AI says 2pm. If you change the document to 3pm, the AI says 3pm within 2 minutes. It never invents — it only retrieves."

**"Does our data go to OpenAI?"**
> "The text of your question and the relevant document excerpts are sent to OpenAI for generation. Your full Knowledge Base is never sent. We use the minimum context needed. OpenAI's enterprise API doesn't train on API data."

**"Can you integrate with our PMS?"**
> "Today: CSV import and export. Roadmap: WhatsApp integration, PMS connectors, and Zapier for everything else. What PMS are you using? I'll add it to our prioritization list."

**"How many customers do you have?"**
> Be honest. Early stage. "We're at launch stage — Tuesday is our first commercial push. The technical foundation is production-grade. You'd be a founding customer with direct input into the roadmap."

## What NOT to Do

- **Do NOT refresh the page mid-demo** — chat history disappears
- **Do NOT try to sign up live** — onboarding depends on migrations
- **Do NOT show the /admin panel** — too technical
- **Do NOT demo on a phone** — dashboard has no mobile navigation
- **Do NOT show billing upgrade buttons** unless Stripe is confirmed working
- **Do NOT start without testing the suggested questions work** on your seeded account

## Recovery Moves If Something Breaks

| What breaks | Recovery |
|------------|---------|
| AI chat returns error | Say "Let me try a different question" — may be a rate limit or brief outage. Reload. |
| Page won't load | Open the landing page instead. Walk through the features visually. |
| Streaming seems slow | Acknowledge: "This is a live call to OpenAI. Real-world speed." |
| Visitor asks about a feature that doesn't exist | "That's on our roadmap. We're building it based on customer input. What matters most to you?" |
